The gold standard for beginner Japanese learners. Genki I covers JLPT N5, and Genki II covers most of N4. Each lesson includes grammar explanations, vocabulary, kanji, and practice exercises.

## Why it's recommended
- Clear, organized structure
- Balanced approach to all skills (reading, writing, listening, speaking)
- Comprehensive grammar explanations
- Abundant practice exercises
- Widely used, so many online resources available

## How to use it
- Complete one lesson per week (3-4 hours per lesson)
- Do all exercises, not just the ones you like
- Review vocabulary daily with Anki
- Practice listening with the audio CDs
- Keep a notebook for new kanji
