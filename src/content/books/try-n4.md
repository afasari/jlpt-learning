Part of the popular Try! series, this book focuses specifically on N4 grammar with clear explanations and abundant practice exercises.

## Why it's recommended
- Covers all N4 grammar points in detail
- Clear explanations in English and Japanese
- Abundant practice problems
- Two practice tests included
- Suitable for self-study

## How to use it
- Study one grammar point per day (2-3 points per week)
- Read the explanation carefully
- Do all the practice exercises
- Make Anki cards for difficult patterns
- Create your own sentences using each grammar point
