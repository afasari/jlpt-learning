## Meaning
Topic marker - "As for X" or "Speaking of X"

## Usage
Noun + は + predicate

## Examples
- **これはペンです。**
  (Kore wa pen desu.)
  This is a pen.

- **わたしは学生です。**
  (Watashi wa gakusei desu.)
  I am a student.

- **田中さんは日本人です。**
  (Tanaka-san wa Nihonjin desu.)
  Mr./Ms. Tanaka is Japanese.

## Notes
- は marks the topic of conversation - what you're talking about
- Often omitted in casual speech
- Different from が (ga) which marks the subject
- Use は when introducing a new topic
