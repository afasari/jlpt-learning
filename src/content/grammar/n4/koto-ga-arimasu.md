## Meaning
"Sometimes [X]" or "There are times when [X]" - Used to express that something happens occasionally.

## Usage
Verb-dictionary form + ことがあります
Verb-negative form + ことがあります

## Examples
- **たまに日本に行くことがあります。**
  (Tamani Nihon ni iku koto ga arimasu.)
  I sometimes go to Japan.

- **雨の日は家にいることがあります。**
  (Ame no hi wa ie ni iru koto ga arimasu.)
  There are times when I stay at home on rainy days.

- **夜遅くまで仕事をすることがあります。**
  (Yoru osoku made shigoto o suru koto ga arimasu.)
  Sometimes I work until late at night.

## Notes
- Different from "koto ga arimasu" meaning "have experienced [X]" (past tense of "koto ga aru")
- Use "tamani" (sometimes) or "tokidoki" (occasionally) together for emphasis
- Often used with "mae" (before) in "～ことがあります" meaning "it sometimes happens that..."
